FAQ

  • You can register for both, but you can attend only one if selected.
  • Who can attend inGenius 2019?
  • inGenius is open to all college students from any year, branch or degree.
  • Is there a registration fee?
  • Yes, the registration fee is Rs. 400 per person for both events.
  • How many people can I have in my team for inGenius?
  • You can have a team size of 2 or 3. Teams can comprise of students from different years, departments and even different colleges.
  • How many people can I have in my team for GirlGeeksHack?
  • You can have 2-4 people in your team for GirlGeeksHack. Teams can comprise of working professionals or students. Minimum 50% of team members must be women.

  • The theme will be announced shortly.
  • What is the theme of Girl Geeks Hack?
  • The theme will be announced shortly.
  • When will the hackathon begin?
  • The hackathon begins on November 16th at 9 AM and continues for 24 hours.
  • What should I submit at the end of the hackathon?
  • You will have to show a working proof of concept of your idea.
  • How will you judge?
  • The panel of judges will consist of industry experts and professionals. They will go through the executable file and will have an interview round with top 10 teams to decide on final evaluations.

ACM


The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is a US based international learned society for computing, and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society.

ACM Student Chapter here at PES University Electronic City Campus brings you a world of opportunities, events and contains a wide range of activities to keep ACM moving including organizing conferences, improving Technical skills, Networking, and we strive to provide a professional experience to our members.

Code of Conduct

Organised by the PESIT South IEEE Computer Society, inGenius is our annual inter-college, 24 hour hackathon.

We are a platform for students pursuing their professional degree,to apply knowledge gained in the classroom and prototype meaningful products.

We value the participation of every member and want all attendees to have an enjoyable and rewarding experience. Accordingly, every attendee of the event is expected to show respect and courtesy to every other attendee throughout the event.

To make clear what is expected, all delegates/attendees, speakers, exhibitors, organizers and volunteers at inGenius 2019 are required to conform to the following Code of Conduct.

Organizers will enforce this code throughout the event.

inGenius is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, sexual orientation, physical appearance, disability, race, religion or employment. We don't tolerate harassment of attendees in any form.

All communication should be appropriate for a professional audience, including people from many different backgrounds.

Code for Sponsors:
Sponsors or vendor booths, are subject to the anti-harassment policy. Booth staff (including volunteers) should not use sexualized clothing/uniforms/costumes, or otherwise create a sexualized environment.

Code for Judges:
Sexual language or imagery is inappropriate and refrain from using sexist, racist or exclusionary language in your content.

Code for Participants:
Be kind and sensitive to the people around you and avoid any kind of offensive behavior. Sexist, racist or any other form of exclusionary or offensive jokes or excessive public swearing are not appropriate at the event.
Attendees violating these rules may be asked to leave the event without a refund at the sole discretion of the organizers. Thank you for your consideration and help in making inGenius 2019 a welcoming, friendly event for all of us.

girlGeeksHack

About girlGeeksHack

Objective Encourage leadership and participation by young women.

Team Size Team size of 2-4. A minimum of 50% of the team must be women participants.

Eligibility Open to both students and working professionals.

Theme The hackathon theme will be announced soon.

Mode of hackathon It is a 24-hour hackathon with 2-3 rounds of judging. The top 10 teams will be selected to pitch the idea on the stage to everyone.

Judges Our panel of judges comprises of alumni, industry experts and top academics. They will evaluate submissions and have an interview round with top 10 teams to decide on final result.

Registration fees If your team is shortlisted, then the registration fee is INR 400 per person. (Payment is not needed to sign up with your team)

CompSoc

The IEEE Computer Society is the world's leading membership organization dedicated to computer science and technology. Serving more than 60,000 members, the IEEE Computer Society is the trusted information, networking, and career-development source for a global community of technology leaders that includes researchers, educators, software engineers, IT professionals, employers, and students.



IEEE PESITSouth Computer Society has been started in 2017 by the Computer Science Department of PESIT South Campus. We also organise workshops, seminars and a couple of amazing events like inGenius (annual) and the Pursuit (bi-annual Online Treasure Hunt)!
